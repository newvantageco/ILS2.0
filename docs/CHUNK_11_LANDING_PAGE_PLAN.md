## Design Guidelines

### Colors
- **Primary:** Your brand blue (#0066CC)
- **Secondary:** Accent green (#00CC66)
- **Background:** Clean white (#FFFFFF)
- **Text:** Dark gray (#333333)
- **Muted:** Light gray (#F5F5F5)

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, large (48-72px)
- **Subheadlines:** Medium weight (24-32px)
- **Body:** Regular weight (16-18px)
- **CTAs:** Bold, uppercase (14-16px)

### Spacing
- **Section padding:** 80-120px vertical
- **Element spacing:** 24-48px between elements
- **Container max-width:** 1200px

### CTAs
- **Primary:** Solid color, high contrast
- **Secondary:** Outline or ghost button
- **Size:** Large, clickable (48px height minimum)
