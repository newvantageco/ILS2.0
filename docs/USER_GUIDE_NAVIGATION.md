# User Guide: Navigation, Logout, and Role Switching

## Quick Reference

### How to Navigate
1. **Using Sidebar Menu**: Click any menu item in the left sidebar
2. **Page Loading**: You'll see a loading spinner while the page loads
3. **If Error Occurs**: A "Reload Page" button will appear - click it to retry

### How to Logout
1. Click the **logout icon** (door with arrow) in the top-right corner
2. You'll be automatically redirected to the login page
3. Your session will be completely cleared

### How to Switch Roles
1. Click the **role button** in the top-right corner (shows your current role)
2. Select the role you want to switch to from the dropdown
3. You'll see a loading spinner briefly
4. The page will reload with your new role and permissions

## Troubleshooting

### "Page Not Loading" or Blank Screen
**Fix**: 
- Click the browser refresh button (F5 or Cmd+R)
- If error message appears, click "Reload Page"
- Check your internet connection

### Logout Not Working
**Fix**:
- Try clicking logout again
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Close and reopen the browser

### Role Switching Stuck
**Fix**:
- Wait 5 seconds and refresh the page
- The role change will still be saved
- Try switching roles again

### Lost Session After Idle Time
**Normal Behavior**: Sessions expire after inactivity
**Fix**: Simply login again

## Best Practices

### ✅ Do
- Logout when leaving your computer
- Wait for pages to fully load before navigating
- Use the role switcher only when needed
- Keep your browser up to date

### ❌ Don't
- Click navigation links multiple times rapidly
- Close browser during role switch
- Use browser back button immediately after role switch
- Have multiple tabs open with different roles

## Getting Help

If you experience persistent issues:
1. Take a screenshot of any error messages
2. Note the steps you took before the issue
3. Contact your system administrator
4. Include your role and which page had the issue
