## üéì Design Patterns Used

### 1. Social Proof Layering
- Quantitative (metrics)
- Visual (logos)
- Emotional (testimonials)
- Authoritative (compliance badges)

### 2. Progressive Disclosure
- Tabbed features reduce cognitive load
- Users explore what matters to them
- Information architecture scales

### 3. Trust Pyramid
- **Top:** Immediate visual trust (logos)
- **Middle:** Functional proof (features + screenshots)
- **Base:** Emotional connection (testimonials)
- **Foundation:** Security assurance (compliance)

### 4. Dual Conversion Paths
- **Self-Serve:** Free trial (low friction)
- **High-Touch:** Book demo (larger practices)

### 5. Benefit Hierarchy
- **Hero:** Ultimate outcome (transform your practice)
- **Speed:** Operational efficiency (save time)
- **Features:** Specific capabilities (what you can do)
- **Social Proof:** Real results (what others achieved)
